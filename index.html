Welcome to Lucky Bal Niketan

Sh. B.L. Kachhwaha
Our Founder

The school was established in 1977 by holy hands of Mr. B.L. Kachhwaha. It is an English Medium Co-Educational school affiliated to Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan. Being one of the oldest names amongst the pioneer educational institutes of Suncity, we firmly believe in achieving excellence with no compromise on quality standards and performance. Our professionally trained, qualified and dedicated team of mentors, backed-up by latest teaching techniques and tools is fully functional to create an adorable learning environment so that children love coming to school with no fear of studies. In order to create such an environment we provide a blend of traditional teaching with smart audio-visuals which makes learning a fun.

Keeping in mind the all-round development of the children, outdoor activities like Picnic, Educational Tour, Cricket and Football Tournaments as well as indoor games like Carrom-Board, Chess and competitions like Fancy Dress, Dance, Art & Craft, Debate, Quiz etc. are organized at regular interval. Such activities definitely boost-up their eternal strength and creativity.
